# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: 2026-03-29**

At WiFi Coin Wallet, we value your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, storage, and protection of your data, particularly in relation to WiFi data tracking, Firebase authentication, cloud synchronization, user data protection, and compliance with international privacy regulations.

## 1. Information We Collect

### WiFi Data Tracking
We collect information about your WiFi networks and usage to provide location-based services and improve user experience. This data may include:
- SSID of your WiFi networks
- Signal strength
- Connection duration
- Data usage statistics

### Firebase Authentication
To authenticate users, we use Firebase authentication services. This involves collecting:
- Email addresses
- Phone numbers (if linked)
- User-generated passwords

### User Data Protection
We take necessary measures to protect user data from unauthorized access and misuse. This includes:
- Encryption of sensitive data
- Regular security audits

## 2. Data Use
We use the collected information for the following purposes:
- To provide, maintain, and improve our services
- To communicate with you, including sending updates and notifications
- To analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience

## 3. Cloud Synchronization
All user data may be synchronized in the cloud to ensure seamless access across devices. This data is encrypted and protected according to industry standards.

## 4. Compliance with International Privacy Regulations
We comply with applicable international privacy regulations, including:
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- Other relevant privacy laws

## 5. Your Rights
You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Request correction of your data
- Object to the processing of your data
- Request deletion of your information

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
